Open Dossier · living figures · the Cosmic Zoom seam

From Mercury to the Milky Way, in one fall

One continuous zoom that composes two figures — the Keplerian orrery and the procedural galaxy — crossfading through the interstellar void with the real nearest stars as honest waypoints. Rendered by figures/cosmiczoom.js, which calls renderOrrery + renderGalaxy (it does not reimplement them).

Press Journey to fall outward (Powers-of-Ten style), or drag the cosmic-zoom slider / pick a waypoint. Watch the Sun stay put at screen center as the frame inverts — from "the Sun is the center of everything" (orrery) to "the Sun is one dot in a spur" (galaxy). The vast gap between the Oort cloud and the nearest stars is labeled, not blank: the honest void.
